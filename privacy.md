  - Google Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy)

  Information may be processed for the following purposes:

  - Providing game features and user settings
  - Providing personalized or non-personalized advertisements

  - Diagnosing errors and improving app performance
  - Complying with applicable laws and platform policies

  Settings → Privacy Options

  Game scores and settings are stored locally on your device. They may be removed when you uninstall the app.

  You may contact us using the details below if you have questions or requests concerning your information.

  Neon Sync Rush does not knowingly collect personal information from children below the applicable age of digital consent without

  If you believe that a child’s personal information has been processed improperly, contact us so that we can investigate and take

  ## 8. Your Rights

  Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to:

  - Confirm whether your personal information is being processed
  - Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information
  - Withdraw consent for data processing or personalized advertising
