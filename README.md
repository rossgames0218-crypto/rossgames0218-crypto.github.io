# Neon Sync Rush

Neon Sync Rush is a casual rhythm game built around futuristic neon visuals and original procedural electronic music.

Choose a track, select a difficulty, and tap each lane as notes reach the glowing hit line. Perfect, Great, Good, and Miss judgments appear instantly, helping you improve your timing and push your combo higher with every run.

Key features:
- 4-lane tap rhythm gameplay
- Easy, Normal, and Hard difficulty levels
- 3 tracks: Neon Steps, Pixel Dash, and Future Glow
- Original procedural electronic music
- Perfect, Great, Good, and Miss judgment system
- Combo, accuracy, rank, and high score tracking
- Input offset calibration
- Music volume, sound effect volume, and haptic settings
- Neon flashes and cyber-style hit effects
- Offline play with no network connection required

Neon Sync Rush focuses on quick rhythm sessions without complex progression systems. Pick a song, jump into the beat, and keep improving your score one run at a time.

The music included in the app does not use external commercial tracks. It is generated by the app's internal procedural audio logic as original gameplay music.
